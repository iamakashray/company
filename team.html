Biography

Akash Ray

Founder • CEO (Chief Executive Officer) • Senior Editor

Akash Ray is a student and a passionate editor who started his journey by learning editing step-by-step through practice. He focuses on clean retouching, cinematic color, sharp captions, and platform-ready delivery. His editing style is simple: keep it natural, make it premium, and match the client’s vision.

Over time, he worked on different types of edits—photos, reels, short videos, and branded content—and developed a workflow that is fast, organized, and client-friendly. He believes good editing is not just effects; it’s timing, clarity and emotion.

Co-Founder • Marketing & Client Relations

Bibhas Roy

Co-Founder • Senior Editor • Creative Director

Bibhas Roy is a Co-Founder, Senior Editor and Creative Director at Creator Hub. He leads the creative direction of the company, ensuring every project maintains a strong visual identity and high editing standards.
